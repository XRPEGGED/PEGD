What is RANSEM?

RANSEM (RNSM) is a community token built around the XRP ecosystem and the legendary figure known as Ansem — one of crypto's most influential voices for XRP and Solana.


The name is a mashup: Ransom + Ansem — a nod to the cult following that formed around his calls. RANSEM launched on Solana, backed by the XRPEGGED treasury, with 150 million tokens airdropped free to verified XRP holders.


It's not just a meme. The XRPEGGED compute pool backs the token with real hashrate, and PEGD buybacks create organic demand. RANSEM is the community layer on top.
