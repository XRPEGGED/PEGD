Frequently Asked Questions

The Marketplace

What is PEGD Market?

PEGD Market is a crypto-native marketplace where real, physical and digital goods are listed at USD prices. Buyers pay using PEGD (preferred), XRP via Xaman, or SOL via Phantom. There are no hidden fees — the listed price is what you pay in token equivalent at checkout.

What are "real items"?

Real items are tangible physical goods (collectibles, merchandise, electronics, etc.) or verifiable digital goods (NFTs with provable on-chain ownership, license keys, etc.) listed by sellers on the marketplace. XRPEGGED does not manufacture or stock goods — all listings are from individual sellers.

How does shipping work?

Shipping is arranged between the buyer and seller after checkout. Estimated delivery times and shipping regions are listed per item where applicable. XRPEGGED is not a party to the shipping contract and does not operate a warehouse or fulfillment center. Buyers should confirm shipping details directly with the seller before purchasing.

What is your return and refund policy?

Returns and refunds are handled between buyer and seller on a per-transaction basis. Because payments are settled directly on-chain, XRPEGGED cannot reverse or hold transactions on your behalf. Contact the seller first. If you cannot reach the seller, email xrpegged@proton.me and we will assist where possible. As with all e-commerce, there is inherent risk — buy from sellers with verifiable history and start with lower-value items if you are new.

Are there buyer protections?

On-chain payments are irreversible by design. XRPEGGED does not offer a chargeback mechanism. We publish completed order records publicly so you can assess seller reliability before purchasing. We recommend treating PEGD Market purchases like any peer-to-peer marketplace transaction — exercise normal caution.

What is Proof of Worth?

Proof of Worth is our public record of completed marketplace orders. Every fulfilled transaction is listed at market.pegd.org/proof so anyone can verify that real goods have changed hands on the platform. Check a seller's order history there before buying — no account required.

The PEGD Token

What is PEGD?

PEGD is an SPL token on Solana. It functions as the preferred payment currency for PEGD Market. It is a utility token — not an investment product, not a security, and not a store of value. Acquire PEGD only in the amount you intend to spend on marketplace purchases.

Is PEGD an investment?

No. There is no promise or expectation of profit from holding PEGD. Its value may fluctuate or go to zero. See the full Disclaimer for details.

How do I get PEGD?

  • Buy: Connect your Phantom wallet on Jupiter and swap any Solana token for PEGD instantly.
  • Earn by mining: Run the PEGD worker on any GPU or CPU. Your hashrate earns a share of the compute pool, paid out in PEGD every 3 hours. See the Mine page to get started.

The Treasury

What is the XRP treasury?

A portion of marketplace revenue is held in XRP on the XRP Ledger as a publicly verifiable reserve. The treasury wallet address is rPEGGED33W7WnBkLKwb1aLMaWvw5cbWX78. Anyone can view the live balance on Bithomp or the XRPL explorer at any time — no login required.

How do I verify the treasury on-chain?

  • XRP: Visit Bithomp and enter the treasury wallet address.
  • Solana: View the compute pool wallet on Solscan.
  • The Treasury section on the homepage also shows live balances pulled directly from the blockchain.

How is treasury revenue used?

Revenue from marketplace sales funds PEGD compute pool payouts and ongoing site operations. Treasury moves require Chairman sign-off and are recorded on-chain. There is no off-chain slush fund — all balances are publicly verifiable.

Mining / Compute Pool

How does the compute pool work?

Run the PEGD worker on any machine with a GPU or CPU. Your machine mines through Unmineable under the PEGD pool — Unmineable tracks your share of the total work contributed (measured in share-seconds). Every 3 hours the treasury distributes $PEGD directly to miners proportional to their share-seconds. Download the worker from the Mine page.

Where does the treasury PEGD come from?

Mining earns SOL via Unmineable, which accumulates in the treasury. The system automatically buys $PEGD with that SOL when the price dips — specifically, when the current PEGD/SOL rate is 10% more favourable than the recent average, or when 0.1 SOL has accumulated regardless of price. This means the treasury refills at better-than-average prices over time. You can watch the live SOL balance and buyback status on the Pool page.

What algorithm should I mine with?

The worker selects the most profitable algorithm automatically. If you are configuring manually: NVIDIA and AMD GPUs work best with KawPow or Ethash; CPU-only machines should use RandomX. All algorithms pay out to the same PEGD pool.

Is mining payout guaranteed?

No. Payouts depend on contributed hashrate, network conditions, and the current profitability of the assigned coin. Mining rewards are variable. Always review the worker source code before running it on your hardware.

Why didn't I receive my first payout?

PEGD is a Solana token. Before any Solana token can land in your wallet, that wallet must have an Associated Token Account (ATA) for PEGD — a small on-chain account that holds your balance. If you have never received PEGD before, your ATA may not exist yet, which causes the first payout transfer to be skipped.

To create your PEGD token account: open Phantom, go to Manage token list, search for PEGD (mint BKSHGmoZ16nCGSLbgRWcXc9qPZGgpufZ4kX3PJc1moon), and add it. Alternatively, swap any amount of SOL for PEGD on Jupiter — this creates the account automatically. Your accumulated earnings will arrive on the next 3-hour cycle.
