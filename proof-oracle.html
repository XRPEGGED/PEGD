🔍 What Happens Each Escrow Release
  • 1B XRP escrow finishes on the first of the month via EscrowFinish (proof is on-ledger).
  • Unused XRP is re-escrowed into new future contracts; the remainder is available for distribution.
  • Distribution flows go through known Ripple wallets (escrow, distribution, OTC) before leaving Ripple control.
  • Nothing is burned in this process; only transaction fees are permanently destroyed.
  • Each release has a transaction hash, amount released, amount re-locked, and net distribution you can link to.
When ledger proofs are fetched, timeline items show “VERIFIED” with links; otherwise values are estimated.
