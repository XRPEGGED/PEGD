How It Works

Step 01
Register
Connect your Solana wallet, verify your XRP balance on-chain, and complete bonus tasks to increase your share of the 150M RNSM pool.
Step 02
Snapshot
Registration closes. Every XRP address is re-verified on-chain — your allocation uses whichever is lower, your registered balance or your balance at snapshot. Hold your XRP to keep your full share. PEGD holdings are also re-checked.
Step 03
Airdrop
RNSM sent directly from treasury wallet to your registered Solana address. No claiming, no gas fees on your end.
